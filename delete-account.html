Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your SkateQuest account and all associated data at any time.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your XP, achievements, crew memberships, and data will be permanently removed.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and username
  • All XP, levels, and achievements
  • Crew memberships and crew data you created
  • Check-ins, trick logs, and session history
  • Videos and clips you uploaded
  • All personal data associated with your account

How to delete your account

1

Open SkateQuest on your device

2

Go to your Profile tab

3

Tap Settings → Account → Delete Account

4

Confirm deletion — your account will be permanently removed within 24 hours

Delete by email

If you can't access the app, email us with the subject "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account. We'll process your request within 5 business days.
