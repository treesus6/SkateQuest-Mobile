## The Problem

**30 million skateboarders worldwide lack a unified platform to:**

- Discover quality skate spots in their area
- Connect with the local skateboarding community
- Track their progress and achievements
- Access skateboarding resources and support

**Additional Social Impact:**

- 15% of youth skateboarders come from underserved communities
- Many talented kids can't afford equipment ($100-300 for a complete setup)
- No existing platform connects skate culture with charitable giving

---

## The Solution: SkateQuest

**A comprehensive Progressive Web App (PWA) that transforms skateboarding culture into a connected, gamified community while creating real social impact.**

### Core Value Propositions:

1. **Community Discovery** - Interactive map of 10,000+ skate spots worldwide
2. **Social Gaming** - XP, achievements, crews, leaderboards
3. **Event Organization** - Local meetups, competitions, and sessions
4. **Social Impact** - Charity QR scavenger hunts that fund skateboards for underprivileged youth

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams:

**Phase 1 (Current - Demo):**

- Platform building and user acquisition
- Charity system proof-of-concept

**Phase 2 (Launch):**

1. **Freemium Subscription**
   - Free: Basic features, ads
   - Premium ($4.99/mo): Ad-free, exclusive challenges, advanced stats
   - Pro ($9.99/mo): Event hosting, crew analytics, video storage
   - **Target:** 5% conversion = $30K MRR at 50K users

2. **Charity QR Codes**
   - Users purchase codes ($2-50) to hide around cities
   - 10% platform fee for processing/operations
   - 90% goes directly to buying skateboards for kids
   - **Target:** $10K/mo in donations = $1K platform revenue

3. **Sponsored Challenges**
   - Brands sponsor trick challenges ($500-5000/challenge)
   - Red Bull, Monster, Vans, Element, etc.
   - **Target:** 20 challenges/mo = $20K MRR

4. **Shop Partnerships**
   - Featured shop listings ($100/mo)
   - Affiliate commissions (5-10% on gear sales)
   - **Target:** 50 shops = $5K MRR

5. **Event Ticketing**
   - 5% commission on paid event registrations
   - Premium event hosting features
   - **Target:** $2K MRR

**Total Year 1 Revenue Target:** $600K ($50K/mo by Month 12)
