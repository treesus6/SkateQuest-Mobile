Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Introduction

SkateQuest is a skateboarding community app with 27,000+ skatepark locations, XP progression, crew systems, video challenges, skate games, and social features. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

You Provide

  • Account: username, email, encrypted password
  • Profile: display name, bio, skill level, avatar, home park
  • Content: videos, photos, comments, ratings, challenge submissions
  • Social: crew memberships, messages, event RSVPs
  • Activity: trick logs, session records, game scores, XP, quests
  • Location: GPS when you check in, add spots, or search nearby parks

Automatically Collected

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • Features used, screens visited, session duration
  • Crash reports via Sentry (no personal data)

How We Use It

  • Power the map, trick tracking, crew battles, challenges, and XP system
  • Show nearby skateparks and spots
  • Enable crews, lounge chat, activity feeds, and notifications
  • Track badges, achievements, and rewards
  • Skate shop check-ins and local shop discovery
  • Detect abuse and improve the app

Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. Ever.

  • Supabase: Secure database and file storage
  • Mapbox: Map rendering and location services
  • Sentry: Crash reporting — no personal data transmitted
  • Expo/EAS: App delivery and OTA updates

Your username, profile, videos, and spot contributions are visible to other users. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect user safety.

Camera, Microphone & Media

Only used when you actively record or upload. Never accessed in the background. Media only shared when you choose to post it.

Location

Only active when you use map features. Never tracked in the background or when the app is closed. Never sold or shared with advertisers.

Security

  • Passwords encrypted, never stored in plain text
  • HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Row-Level Security on all database tables

Children's Privacy

SkateQuest is not for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, email us immediately and we will delete it.

Your Rights

  • View, edit, or delete your data anytime through your profile
  • Disable location or notifications in device/app settings
  • Request full account deletion at any time
  • California: right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale (we don't sell)
  • EU/GDPR: rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability
