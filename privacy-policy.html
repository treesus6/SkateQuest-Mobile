Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

SkateQuest ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, username, profile photo
  • Location data: approximate location to show nearby skateparks (only when you use the app)
  • Usage data: sessions, tricks logged, challenges completed, XP earned
  • User-generated content: videos, photos, comments you choose to share
  • Device information: device type, operating system, app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the SkateQuest app and its features
  • To show you nearby skateparks and skate shops
  • To enable social features like crews, challenges, and leaderboards
  • To send optional notifications about activity in your crew or challenges
  • To analyze app usage and fix bugs via Sentry error tracking

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertisers
  • We do not track your location when the app is closed

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

SkateQuest is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Mapbox — map rendering
  • Sentry — error tracking and crash reporting
  • Expo — app delivery platform

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, visit: sk8.quest/delete-account
